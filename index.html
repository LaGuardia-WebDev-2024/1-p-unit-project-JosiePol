Animals with white tails

Examples of Animals with White tails

  • White tailed deer
  • Rabbits
  • Mule Deer

    • What do white tails help these animals with

    Each animal uses their white tail with a slightly different but also similar purpose since they all developed white tails as a defense mechanism.
    White Tailed Deer
    The White Tailed Deer uses its tail to signal danger to other members of its herd. This is called "flagging". It also does this to distract predators, making the deer less obvious to predators.
    Mule Deer
    The Mule Deer uses its tail to signal to other deers that there is danger. However, unlike the White Tailed Deer, it does not help it in running away.
    Rabbits
    Rabbits use their tails to communicate danger to other rabbits and to confuse predators when running be seeming to disapear.

    Where do animals with white tails live

    These animals mainly live in ther North East region of America
