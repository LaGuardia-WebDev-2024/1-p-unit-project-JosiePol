    • What do white tails help these animals with

    Each animal uses their white tail with a slightly different purpose
    White Tailed Deer
    The White Tailed Deer uses its tail to signal danger to other members of its herd. This is called "flagging". It also does this to distract predators, making the deer less obvious to predators.
    Mule Deer
    The Mule Deer uses its tail to signal to other deers that there is danger. However, unlike the White Tailed Deer, it does not help it in running away.
